Our Story

Aso Luxe was born from a simple frustration: the continent had centuries of the world's most vibrant textile traditions from Ankara to Kente to Adire, yet everyday streetwear rarely celebrated them.

Founded in Mauritius in 2025 by a group of young creatives, Aso Luxe set out to change that. We design pieces rooted in African print culture but engineered for modern life; whether you're heading to a lecture, a rooftop dinner, or a beach party on the east coast.

Our name comes from the Yoruba word Aso, meaning "cloth" or "fabric", a tribute to the thread that ties us all together. Every drop is limited, every piece intentional.

What We Stand For

Pan-African Pride

Every print we use carries the memory of generations of African artisans. We honour that lineage with every stitch.

Crafted to Last

Fast fashion is the enemy of culture. We make limited, quality drops that outlast seasons and trends.

Built for Youth

Students and young professionals deserve luxury at accessible prices. Aso Luxe bridges that gap.

Conscious Production

Small batches, local sourcing where possible, and zero waste packaging. The earth matters to us.
